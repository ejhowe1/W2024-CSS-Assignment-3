Reese's Pieces Peanut Butter Cups

Reese's Pieces Peanut Butter Cups are a delicious combination of smooth milk chocolate and creamy peanut butter. The perfect treat for any time of day.

Interesting Facts About Reese's Peanut Butter Cups

  • Reese's Pieces were introduced in 1978 and gained widespread popularity after being featured in the movie "E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial".
  • Originally, Reese's Pieces were only available in peanut butter flavor, but over the years, new flavors such as chocolate, peanut butter, and candy shell have been introduced.
  • The iconic orange, yellow, and brown colors of Reese's Pieces were chosen to differentiate them from other candies on the market.
  • Reese's Pieces are manufactured by The Hershey Company, which also produces other popular candy brands like Hershey's chocolate bars and Kit Kat.
  • Each serving of Reese's Pieces contains about 19 pieces and provides a delicious combination of sweet and salty flavors.
